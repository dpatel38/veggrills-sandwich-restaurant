About Us

OUR STORY

Veg Grills Sandwiches was established in 2018, by Damini Patel, in the city of Charlotte. She started selling Sandwiches and Snacks at a small store. By combining her passion for making sandwiches with excellent business skills, the small store, soon flourished into a full grown business. For starting this year, Veg Grills has been delivering on its promise of 'Unmatched Quality, Unmatched Taste'. Overtime our sandwiches are deeply adored and relished by our customers.

Vision

Our vision is to be a world-renowned food brand that delivers the best in Food Quality, Taste and Hygiene to each and every customer across nations.

Mission

Our goal is to create a fusion of global tastes and serve the customer a piece of every culture along with building for ourselves a reputation of excellent quality and service.

Core Values
